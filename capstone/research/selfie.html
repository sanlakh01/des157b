Davis Centennial Seal

Santhoshi Ramanathan

I love the design and construction of the seal. It showcases the history of Davis in a way that is so uniquely Davis - artistic, thoughtful, and welcoming, with a healthy dash of bikes. I enjoyed how the seal looks like a bike wheel, with the spokes dividing up each section. I also enjoyed that the seal doesn't look exactly like a bike wheel, there are extra rings and a couple larger sections that disrupt and create a cool shape.
